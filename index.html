Here is what I personally order from these restaurants.

From Happy Bowl, I usually get the White Rice Bowl with kara fried chicken, fried egg, cheese and siracha.

From Chick-Fil-A, I usually get the Spicy Chicken Sandwich Meal with no pickles, add American cheese, large fries, and to drink a large sunjoy (Half sweet tea and half lemonade).

From Salsaritas, I usually get the Burrito with mexican rice, no beans, chicken, with hot salsa.
